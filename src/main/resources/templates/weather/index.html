Weather Impact on Construction

Site Management Considerations

  • Extreme temperatures can affect material performance
  • High winds may prevent crane operations
  • Heavy rain can compromise excavation stability
  • Lightning presents serious safety hazards
  • Snow and ice can delay work and create unsafe conditions

Using Weather Data Effectively

  • Plan daily activities based on forecasts
  • Document weather conditions for project records
  • Schedule weather-sensitive tasks during favorable conditions
  • Prepare contingency plans for severe weather events
  • Consider historical weather patterns for long-term planning
